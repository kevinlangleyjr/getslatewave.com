Slatewave for Obsidian treats your vault as a companion surface to the
editor: same palette, same accents, same temperature. Optimized for
long-form reading and writing rather than dense code.

An entry in the Obsidian Community Themes catalog is in flight; until
then, the clone path above is the install.
