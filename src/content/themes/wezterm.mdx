Slatewave for WezTerm tunes every slot WezTerm exposes — not just the
16 ANSI colors. Compose cursor amber, copy-mode active/inactive
match pair mirrors VSCode's `findMatch`/`findMatchHighlight`, and the
tab-bar chrome matches the VSCode activity bar so the window edges
read as one continuous surface.

The three install shapes (TOML, inline Lua, or full bundle) let you
pick up the palette without inheriting the font/cursor/window
opinions. The full bundle can be composed — call `apply_to_config`
then override individual settings after, and the later assignments
win.
