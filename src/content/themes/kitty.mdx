Slatewave for kitty tunes every slot kitty exposes — not just the
16 ANSI colors. The tab bar uses teal-on-slate for the active tab
and chrome `#21252b` for the bar itself, matching the VSCode activity
bar so the window edges read as one continuous surface. The bell
border is amber, marks `1`/`2`/`3` mirror the editor's
error/warning/info accents (rose, amber, sky), and the macOS / Wayland
titlebars tint to the slate background.

The two install shapes let you opt into the typography opinions or
keep your existing font and window settings. The full bundle composes
— layer your overrides _after_ the include in kitty.conf and the
later values win in kitty's parse order.
