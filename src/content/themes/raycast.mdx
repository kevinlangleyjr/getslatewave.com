Slatewave for Raycast maps onto the full twelve-slot theme schema, so
every accent role — red/orange/yellow/green/blue/purple/magenta — ends
up on a semantically-correct Slatewave color. Teal drives both the
selection bar and the loader spinner, keeping the signature visible on
any view.

Custom themes require a Raycast Pro subscription; the theme itself is
a plain JSON blob under the WTFPL, so forks and local overrides stay
trivial.
